About Us

We are a passionate, volunteer-driven animal rescue dedicated to giving cats and dogs a second chance at life. Based in the Greater Houston area and incorporated in Las Vegas, we are proud to be a recognized 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization, which means your generous donations are fully tax deductible.

At our core, we believe every animal deserves safety, love, and a forever home. Whether it's providing shelter, medical care, or rehoming, we work tirelessly to ensure every rescue gets the compassion and support they need. Our team shares a deep love for animals and a commitment to protecting the most vulnerable among us.

Our leadership team brings heart and dedication to everything we do:

  • Svetlana Walters — Founder & President
  • Aaron Walters — Vice President
  • Irina Korotaeva — Treasurer
  • Tatiana Gray — Secretary

Join us in making a difference. Whether you adopt, volunteer, or donate, your support helps us save lives and create happy endings for countless animals in need. Together, we strive to make a meaningful difference—one rescue at a time.
